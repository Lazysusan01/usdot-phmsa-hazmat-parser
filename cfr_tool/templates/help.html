Packaging Regulations Lookup Tool (DOT/CFR)

This tool allows you to search up a hazardous chemical, either by name or UN number. It then returns the packaging information for transporting that chemical in the U.S.

  1. Type either UN{0000}, {0000}, or search the chemicalâ€™s name and click on it.
  2. You can choose either bulk or non-bulk packaging by the checkbox.
  3. Hit enter or click search.
  4. If everything works, this should be the expected result, any errors should flash up with what went wrong.
  5. On the left are the authorized packaging methods, with the packaging codes highlighted in yellow.
  6. Clicking the highlighted packaging codes should then show the specifics for that packaging on the right. i.e, the construction methods, materials, thicknesses etc.
